### Approved Decisions

✅ **Mission Statement**

- **"Build trustworthy software faster. We help your team master AI—not replace humans with it—and create SDLCs that actually sustain."**

✅ **Core Values**

1. **Human-First** - Software serves humans. Tools (including AI) are means, not ends.
2. **Engineering Excellence** - Quality, security, and performance are architectural decisions, not afterthoughts.
3. **Sustainable Velocity** - Speed that compounds over time, not at the cost of team burnout or technical debt.
4. **Trust Through Reproducibility** - Auditable, documented processes so you can trust what you're shipping.
5. **Responsibility** - We're accountable. What we build affects users and teams. That matters.
