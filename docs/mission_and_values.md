## ðŸ’Ž Core Values

### 1. **Human-First**

Software serves humans. Tools (including AI) are means, not ends.

- We prioritize building for end users, not just shipping features
- AI augments human capability, not replaces it
- Team wellbeing matters as much as delivery speed
- We empower people to make informed decisions, not automate them away

### 2. **Engineering Excellence**

Quality, security, and performance are architectural decisions, not afterthoughts.

- We don't compromise on fundamentals
- Code reviews, testing, and security are built into the process from day one
- Technical debt is tracked and addressed systematically
- We train teams to think like engineers, not just coders

### 3. **Sustainable Velocity**

Speed that compounds over time, not at the cost of team burnout or technical debt.

- We measure success by long-term delivery rate, not sprint velocity
- We optimize for team morale and retention
- We build processes that scale without exponential complexity
- We celebrate steady improvement, not heroic rushes

### 4. **Trust Through Reproducibility**

Auditable, documented processes so you can trust what you're shipping.

- Every decision is documented and reasoned
- Processes are repeatable and teachable
- We leave teams with systems they understand and can maintain
- Transparency builds confidence

### 5. **Responsibility**

We're accountable. What we build affects users and teams. That matters.

- We care about the downstream impact of our code and decisions
- We don't hide behind "that's what the client asked for"
- We speak up when we see risk
- We build for the long term, not the quick exit
